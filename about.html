Our Story

What is Heart Warriors?

Heart Warriors is an official health program of the Local Government Unit of Bago City, Negros Occidental. Established in 2021, the program was created in response to the growing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases among Bago City residents, particularly among the elderly and low-income communities.

The program provides free cardiovascular health services including ECG screenings, blood pressure monitoring, cholesterol testing, medicine assistance, and health education seminars — all at no cost to qualified beneficiaries.

Through partnerships with local health centers, barangay officials, and volunteer medical professionals, Heart Warriors has reached over 1,200 beneficiaries across all 48 barangays of Bago City.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Mission

To provide accessible, comprehensive, and compassionate cardiovascular health services to all residents of Bago City — especially the marginalized and underprivileged — through free medical assistance, health education, and community-based interventions that promote heart health and overall wellness.

🌟

Vision

A Bago City where every resident has access to quality heart health care, where cardiovascular diseases are prevented through education and early detection, and where no one suffers alone because of lack of resources or support from their community and government.

💎

Core Values

Compassion — We serve with genuine care and empathy.

Integrity — We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.

Excellence — We strive for the best health outcomes.

Community — We believe in the power of collective action.

🏛️

LGU Commitment

The Local Government Unit of Bago City fully supports Heart Warriors as a flagship health program, allocating dedicated budget, personnel, and infrastructure. The program operates under the City Health Office with direct oversight from the Office of the Mayor.

What We Offer

Our Services

🫀

ECG Screening

Free electrocardiogram screenings to detect heart irregularities early, available at all partner health centers.

💊

Medicine Assistance

Monthly medicine packs for qualified hypertension and heart disease patients who cannot afford maintenance medications.

🩺

Health Consultations

Free consultations with licensed physicians and cardiologists during scheduled community outreach activities.

📚

Health Education

Regular seminars and workshops on heart-healthy lifestyles, diet, exercise, and stress management for all ages.

🏘️

Community Outreach

Mobile health teams visiting all 48 barangays to bring services directly to residents who cannot travel to health centers.

👴

Senior Care

Specialized care and priority services for senior citizens, including home visits for bedridden elderly patients.

Government Support

LGU Involvement

Heart Warriors operates under the direct supervision of the Bago City Local Government Unit, with full institutional support from the City Mayor's Office and the City Health Office.

The program is funded through the city's annual health budget, supplemented by grants from the Department of Health (DOH) and partnerships with private sector organizations committed to community health.

Barangay Health Workers (BHWs) and Barangay Health Emergency Response Teams (BHERTs) serve as the program's frontline implementers, ensuring services reach every corner of the city.
