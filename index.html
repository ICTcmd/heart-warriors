Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, comprehensive, and compassionate cardiovascular health services to all residents of Bago City — especially the marginalized and underprivileged — through free medical assistance, health education, and community-based interventions.

🌟

Our Vision

A Bago City where every resident has access to quality heart health care, where cardiovascular diseases are prevented through education and early detection, and where no one suffers alone because of lack of resources or support.

💪

Core Values

Compassion — We serve with genuine care.
Integrity — We act with honesty and transparency.
Excellence — We strive for the best outcomes.
Community — We believe in the power of togetherness.

🏛️

LGU Commitment

The Local Government Unit of Bago City fully supports Heart Warriors as a flagship health program, allocating resources, personnel, and infrastructure to ensure sustainable and impactful service delivery to all barangays.
